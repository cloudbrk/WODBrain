Savings as App

These instructions are for an iPhone using Safari. Other phones and browsers will follow similar steps.

  1. Navigate to the WOD Weight page and click the center icon on the Safari browser.

  2. Scroll down until you see the "Add to Home Screen" option, click this.

  3. Re-name the App to what you'd like to see on your homescreen, or leave as is. Then click "Add" in the upper right corner.

  4. Re-arrange your homescreen and show off your sick new app!
