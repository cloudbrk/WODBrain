Founder cloudbreak

Charlie Nolan

Westerly, Rhode Island

Engineer by schooling. Project Manager by profession. Constant learner. Dedicated to continuous improvement.

Cloudbreak was formed as a side project in late 2018 as an outlet for my interest in coding.

2018 was the same year as my introduction to Crossfit. With no background in olympic lifting or excersizing with a barbell I was always scratching my head when a coach would say "pick a weight you can do X reps of unbroken when fresh". I was sure this was sound advice, but how the heck I was supposed to figure that out was another story! I've finally expanded my coding toolset to start making this WODBrain web app.

What has been stored in a spreadsheet on my comptuer is slowly transitioning its way into this app. I hope you like it, and I hope you stick around to watch it grow!
